Code of Conduct/Things you should know

With the registration you should be aware of the following things (and you agree to them):

  1. You should understand the scheduling requirements and you should be able to meet the requirements most of the time.
  2. You have played 10 or more BBC games
  3. You can open the game manually or with writing "/msg bbcbot create husc Player1 vs Player2" in pokerth lobby
  4. Always Enter the results on the website (HUSC->Personal) directly after the game (this should be done by both players)
  5. After that check if your opponents did the same and check if the results are contradicting
  6. If the results are contradicting and you fail to resolve this conflict with your opponent, tell in BBC shoutbox as far as possible, and if you can, send us a logfile or a screenshot of the last hand
  7. If there are complications with the date and time for a game, try talking with your opponent.
  8. You are allowed to play the game at a different time than scheduled. No need to tell the admins in that case.
  9. If your opponent is not there for a game in time, wait 5 minutes.
  10. If your opponent does not appear after 5 minutes, enter the result ("opponent not there") immediately.
  11. If your opponent wasn't there, you can offer him to play at another time (before the round ends), but you don't have to.
  12. If your opponent disconnects during the game, you continue playing until you win.
  13. Be respectful towards others.
  14. Try avoiding chaos, to keep the effort for the admins low.
